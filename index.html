Project Values

Each of my projects center around one, if not all of three core values that I follow.

Communication

I put my best foot forward in my projects so I and my teammates can find ways of connecting with out audience so we can learn and spread their message.

Creativity

Creativity is essential for not only presenting my work in the best way possible, but it is also needed for finding out-of-the-box solutions to nontrivial issues.

Computing

All of my projects have either then inspired by or created on computing devices in an attempt to expand my horizons in both conceptual knowledge and first-hand experience.
